About

I am a Research Scientist at Meta CTRL-Labs. Previously, I worked as an Applied Scientist at Amazon AWS AI Labs on continual learning with foundation models. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Yale University (2024), M.S. from KAIST (2020), and B.S. from Sogang University (2018).

Research Summary

My research focuses on neuromotor interfaces, neuromorphic computing, and efficient model design with practical impact. Key themes include:

Neuromorphic SNN

Spiking neural networks for energy-efficient learning and inference.

Efficient Learning

Strategies that reduce computation and memory while preserving performance.

Continual Learning

Methods that retain knowledge as new data arrives.
