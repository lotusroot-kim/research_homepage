Research Summary

My research focuses on neuromotor interfaces, neuromorphic computing, and efficient model design with practical impact. Key themes include:

Efficient ML

Strategies that reduce computation and memory while preserving performance, with recent focus on LLMs and multimodal LLMs.

Neuromorphic SNN

Spiking neural networks for energy-efficient learning and inference.

AR/VR Adaptation

Model adaptation for dynamically changing conditions on edge devices in AR/VR applications.
